The Best Movies According to Tom

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Fight Club

Fight club is my favorite movie ever. Love the 90s vibe and the acting and story is so great, I have watched it many times and will continue to do so.

Blade Runner 2049

I'm a big fan of the whole dystopian future setting. The story is great, Ryan Gosling plays the character very well.

The Revenant

The Revenant is a true master piece in my opinion.The cinematography is breathtaking, Tom Hardy and leonardo dicaprio are both excellent.
